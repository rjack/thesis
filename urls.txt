				   XLATTICE
			  <http://xlattice.sf.net/>

XLattice is a communications infrastructure for peer-to-peer (p2p) networks.
These networks may be server-assisted. XLattice is a modular system consisting
of components intended to be usable for a wide variety of purposes, ranging
from p2p chat to distributed software development environments.



			KADEMLIA DESIGN SPECIFICATION
       <http://xlattice.sf.net/components/protocol/kademlia/specs.html>

Kademlia is a communications protocol for peer-to-peer networks. It is one of
many versions of a DHT, a Distributed Hash Table.
